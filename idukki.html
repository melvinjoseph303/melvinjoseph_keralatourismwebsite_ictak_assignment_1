Idukki is the most beautiful district of the Kerala state. This place is well known for its beauty and the activities. There is a lot of the activities, places where you are easily go with your family, friends, partners and groups. Some of the famous visiting places in Idukki are an Eravikulam national park that is most visited for the three and leopards. This place is divided in two parts in which one of the enclosed for the wildlife sanctuaries and another one for the flowers growing section. You can also buy the entry tickets online from this place from the official site of Eravikulam national parks. Here is you can easily see the variety of flora and fauna. The flowering park offers the magnificent view of the tea plantation. The beauty of this park increased when the blue flower shows the slope of rolling hills from the flower Neelakurinji. Another place of visiting is Anamudi mountain, here you can do most of the activities related to the hill station like trekking, hiking. There are also so many temples which are the totally based on the pure tradition and culture of India. These places give the peace of mind with a lot of positivity.
