Thiruvananthapuram is the largest city of the Kerala state. It is also the capital of Kerala and sometime calling by the name Trivandrum. It is the most popular city of the Kerala as well as the India. Trivandrum is the 5th most populous urban city of the Kerala. It is also the major hub for the IT section that contributes mostly software exports. One more famous place in Trivandrum is the Techno Park that is the largest technology park in all over Asia in terms of area.There are so many visiting places in Trivandrum that attract tourist so much. One of the popular tourist places is Thiruvanathapuram zoo that has a variety of animals. One more attracting place is the lighthouse beach. It is the visiting beach of the Kerala due to its beauty and awesome look. This beach is popular for the night view. The lighthouse on the seashore is spreading their light in all over direction, but when its light is reflected in the sea water than all the directions is shining with the glittering light.
